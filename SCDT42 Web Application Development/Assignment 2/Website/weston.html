WELCOME TO ALLSTARS SPORTS BAR, WESTON-SUPER-MARE

Situated just a short 2 minute walk from the Grand Pier in Weston Super Mare, Allstars is easy to find and impossible to miss! We're open from 10am till late all week so you can drop by any time.

We have fantastic facilities and serve hot and cold food at great prices, including our NEW Pizza menu!!

Families** & Non members are more than welcome!

Live Sport And Entertainment, Choose Your Channel

Here at Allstars Weston-Super-Mare, we have the facilities to show up to 7 events at any given time in HD. We have 2 large projected HD screens as well as over 20 large screen wall-mounted LCD TVs that you can request your choice of broadcast.
We also have BT Sports HD and Sky HD across all of our large HD TVs and HD projected screens. To supplement those we have access to many European channels, showing games from across the continent and the world as well as home matches. Some of these show games and sports that you just won't find on a normal home Satellite box. Learn More ➤
