Frequently Asked Questions, Take a Look

Hope we Can Help

Below is a list of some frequently asked questions about Allstars Sports Bars, please take a look, as any queries you have may have already been asked.
If you can't find the answer you are looking for, then please send us an email through the contact form at the bottom of the page, we'll be happy to help.

If it's a venue-specific query, feel free to give them a call

Do I have to be a member to get into Allstars?

Non-members are perfectly welcome to any of our venues*

Do I have to be a member to hire a pool table?

Non members are welcome to rent Pool, Snooker & American pool tables at any time, just keep in mind if you have a members discount card you will get discounted table rental!   Learn More ➤

Will my Membership Discount Card work in other Allstars venues?

Yes! Each Venue will vary on prices and offers but your card will work at all Allstars venues, this also includes "Free Entry" to Frames Snooker Club in Weston-Super-Mare.   Learn More ➤

There is a big football match on, but will I still be able to watch the rugby?

All our venues have multiple sky and European boxes, this along with 20+ TVs we can pretty much show everything, We will do our best to accommodate all sporting fans.

Can I bring my Children in?

Children are welcome as long as an adult accompanies them** Please note during big matches it is not advised for younger children (under 12) due to the large numbers of adults consuming alcohol.

Do you show late night sporting events such as Boxing or Wrestling?

Our Venues have a late opening license so we can show live sports that are on late at night/early hours of the morning. You see what we are showing by looking at our Live Sports page or following us on Facebook or Twitter. If you do not see what you are looking for then please just shoot us an email or give us a call.

Do I need ID to be served in Allstars?

Allstars has a "Challenge 25" policy, if you look younger than 25 you may be asked for ID, Our policy (Advised by the local licensing authority) is that we can accept a valid UK driving license, a valid UK passport or a valid "PASS" accredited identification card. Unfortunately we cannot accept any other form of ID including foreign driving license or passports, sorry for any inconvenience.***

Do you have WiFI?

Yes and it's FREE!

I have lost my Membership Discount Card, Can I still get my discounts?

We can find you easily on our system, once we have found you its just a small fee of £1 to replace your card and your good to go!****

*There is a "day membership" fee of £1 for Weston-Super-Mare Allstars.
**Due to local licensing Children under 16 are not allowed in after 9pm, under 18's after 11pm at Taunton Allstars.
***The management has the final decision; if we are not convinced with the ID produced we have the right to not to serve alcohol to anyone.
****Further proof of ID maybe required.
